Most businesses do not have a sales problem. They have a follow-up problem.

Invoices age, staff get busy, phones ring while everyone is working, and the owner ends up carrying the follow-up process manually.

01

03

04

FAQ

Practical questions before you automate customer follow-up.

The audit is built to answer whether a workflow should be automated, what should stay human, and what needs to be tested before customers experience it.

Is this for consumer debt collection?

No. Elixis Collect is positioned for first-party B2B invoice follow-up. It is not for consumer debt collection, medical debt, financial debt, or legal collection work.

Does the AI collect card details?

No. The system should not collect verbal card details. The safer pattern is a professional reminder plus the secure payment path your business already approves.

Can it handle invoices from my current system?

Usually the first audit step is to review how invoice data can be exported, connected, or staged. The exact integration depends on your current tools and permissions.

Can it also answer inbound calls?

Yes. Elixis Intake is the AI receptionist system for missed-call capture, lead qualification, common questions, urgent routing, booking support, and call notes.

What happens when someone disputes an invoice?

The workflow should flag disputes, confusion, and exceptions for human review. It should not negotiate, offer payment plans, or make policy exceptions by default.

Can I review notes and pause outreach?

That is the intended operating model: outcomes stay visible, and outreach rules should include pause, stop, and manual-review paths before launch.

Is SMS available?

SMS should be treated as optional and only used when the business has explicitly enabled and approved that channel. The site does not claim live SMS payment links by default.

What businesses is this for?

Elixis is a fit for service businesses, contractors, property managers, inspection companies, home-service teams, agencies, local operators, and B2B service providers with repeatable follow-up work.
