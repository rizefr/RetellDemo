Questions owners ask

The point is controlled coverage—not blind automation.

Will it replace my office team?

No. The full receptionist is meant to handle approved intake and next steps while your people keep control of exceptions, customer relationships, and decisions that need judgment.

What if the AI gets something wrong?

AI systems can encounter edge cases. Elixis reduces that risk with approved knowledge, scenario tests, explicit boundaries, call summaries, and human routes; it does not claim error-free performance.

Can it quote prices or promise treatment?

Only if you explicitly approve that information and the exact conditions. Otherwise it captures the request and routes the caller to the next approved step.

What does setup cost?

Scope depends on call volume, call types, coverage, booking rules, and integrations. The fit check maps those items before a proposal; this page does not publish an unapproved price.

Does it work with our current software?

The audit reviews your booking, CRM, phone, and notification workflow. Elixis will not promise an integration until the access and technical path are verified.

How do I judge it without testimonials?

Hear the live pest demo, review the test plan, inspect example outputs, and ask Elixis to show which parts are configured, tested, or still dependent on your systems.
