Elixis AI · Collect

Make Unpaid Invoice Follow-Up Consistent

When reminders slip, unpaid invoices sit longer and staff lose time chasing the same accounts. Elixis Collect organizes invoice data and runs approved call-first follow-up with secure payment next steps, clear notes, and human escalation.

What the system works from

Customer list Contact info Contract or service details Unpaid amount Due date Follow-up preferences Escalation rules

When follow-up is inconsistent, cash gets stuck.

Start with the customers or invoices you already know need attention. The system follows your policies, uses approved wording, and logs outcomes so your team has a clear next step.

01

Review the invoice list

Import or review customers, unpaid amounts, due dates, and service context.

02

Segment timing

Identify upcoming-due, recently overdue, and older invoices for different follow-up paths.

03

Approve scripts

Use business-approved wording before any call or supporting payment-link workflow is tested. SMS is only added if explicitly enabled.

04

Run follow-up

Contact customers through the approved sequence and timing windows.

05

Log outcomes

Track paid, promised, disputed, unreachable, or needs-human-review outcomes.

06

Escalate carefully

Escalate only under rules your business approves and applicable policies allow.
